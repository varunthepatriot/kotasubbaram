––––– 5 ENCOUNTERS OF THE ONLY KIND: A COLLECTION OF POEMS pp : 82 Published : 2013 By: Authorspress New Delhi, India About the book Encounters of the Only Kind is the fifth collection of poems of Kota V. Subbaram, Written more than thirty years ago . Encounters of the Only Kind celebrates the voyage of the poet through love in its imaginative , experiential , and reflective facets among others . The form endowed to the poems takes passion to new heights . The uniqueness of the work bathed in erotic idiom makes it an amorous addition to Indo–Anglian poetry. –––––
