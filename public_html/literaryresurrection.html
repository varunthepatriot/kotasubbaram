Toward a Future: IDEAS ON EDUCATION, SCIENCE & SOCIETY
( dedicated to P.N. Haksar, a well-known Indian intellectual )

PP: 126

Published: 1989

By Scorpio Books

  • About Book
  • Content
  • Appendix
  • Review

Toward A Future is a collection of articles presented by the author in various forums on issues concerning education, science and society. The articles, written in a lucid manner in powerful prose, are thought provoking, creating a deep impression on the mind of the reader. The author passionately argues for a rational and humane outlook to pervade and prevail as a living philosophy toward a future for the Indian society.
